RULES

1) Move with the arrow keys 2) Dash with w/a/s/d 3) Player can dash through enemies 4) Avoid enemy projectiles 5) Collect 5 coins to go to next level 6) Getting hit changes player's speed 7) Getting hit 3 times = death 8) Collect hearts to restore health 9) Beware of special enemy types 10) Survive 8 levels to fight boss 11) The boss fight is hard... 12) You won't win...
